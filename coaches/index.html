William Wolf

MTXE Director

William enters his fourth year being director for Team MTXE. Previously, Coach Wolf has been the head coach for 17U and 14U MTXE. In the winter season, he also serves as the head coach for the 8th grade Saint Pius X Basketball team. He has just completed his sixth season coaching 8th grade basketball for St. Pius X. William graduated from St. Pius X in 2006 while playing varsity basketball for 3 years there and winning a region championship his senior year. After high school he obtained a BBA in Marketing from the University of West Georgia graduating in 2011. When its not basketball season, William owns and operates a small business dealing with buying out failing business inventories.

Will Cloyd

6th Grade - 12U

Will is in his third year with MTXE and his third year coaching AAU basketball overall. Coach Cloyd spent his first two years coaching the 6th grade MTXE team and enjoys developing young talent, watching teams come together, and gain chemistry over the season. Last year the 6th grade team advanced out of pool play in every Georgia Cup Tournament they played and finished runner up in the Annual Halftime Sports Mother's Day Tournament. Coach Cloyd hopes to continue helping young players grow and reach their potential as well as experience success both on and off the court.

Email Coach Cloyd

Kevin Bottoms

7th Grade - 13U

Kevin enters his first year as an MTXE coach being named the head coach of the 7th grade team. Coach Bottoms was an accomplished athlete on both the basketball and baseball fronts while attending local Henderson High School (class of 1987) and during his days at the University of George (BBA '91). In his 26 years since departing Athens, Kevin has coached many different boys basketball teams, including several AAU teams that have competed on the local and state level. Kevin and his wife Mabre have four children, three of which are grown and college graduates, and one grandson. Kevin is most passionate about leveraging the game of basketball in the continued development of the character of the young men he has the privilege of leading. In his spare time, Kevin is the Global Vice President of Business Development for TELUS International, which has nearly 30,000 employee's globally.
