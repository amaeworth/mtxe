Tryouts

Teams:

6th, 7th, 8th grade teams
*5th graders may try out for 6th grade

Location:

St. Pius X Catholic School

When:

Sunday, February 11: 7pm - 9pm
Monday, February 12: 7pm - 9pm

Format

After Tryout #1, there will a preliminary cut of players to identify the Top 15-20 players per team that will continue tryouts. A list will be posted on www.teammtxe.org (by Monday, 2/12 at 9:00am) for those who are to return for Tryout #2. Final cuts will be made after Tryout #2. The final rosters of each team will be posted on the website Tuesday, February 13th by 3:00pm.**
